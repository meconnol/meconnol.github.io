Welcome!

Pat the Baker is small town, family run bakery that specializes in breads, pastries, and pretzels. Founded in 2020 during the Covid-19 lockdown orders, Pat Connolly began experimenting with growing his own yeast and making sourdough bread and the rest is history. Check out our menu to find what you like, the about page to learn more about our baking and sustainability goals, and the location page to find where you can pick up Pat the Baker's goods today!

Hand crafted and home-made goods just for you. Formed with patience, love, and fermented yeast of course!
